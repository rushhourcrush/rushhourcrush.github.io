ABOUT

RushHourCrush is a social media tool that allows you to connect with your missed connections. Perhaps you were in a rush when you first met your crush, or maybe just couldn't get your words together in time to take it any steps further, and now the moment is gone, RushHourCrush to the rescue, now it is up to you!

Download the app, check if your crush already posted a message about you or post a new message. Check and write comments, only you, the owner of your crush message can start a private converstation with those who commented your message. You may then choose to reveal your Facebook profile link with your crush.
